## 📝 详细描述（中文版，用于中文商店）

**你是不是也发现，自己越来越懒得思考了？**

问题一冒出来，手比脑子快，"咔"一下就丢给 ChatGPT。
AI 给你完美答案，你点头收下。
半年后你发现——你连这个问题"原本该怎么想"都忘了。

**iThinkAI 就是给你大脑的一个暂停键。**

当你在 ChatGPT、Claude、Gemini 等 AI 平台按下发送时，它会拦下你的问题，弹出一个 10 秒的思考窗口，问你一句：

> 如果是你自己来回答，你会怎么说？

你可以跳过，也可以写下自己的想法。写完再让 AI 回答，两相对比，思考力一点点长回来。

### ✨ 核心功能

🧠 **思考倒计时** — 发送前 10 秒缓冲，节奏你说了算（3-60 秒可调）
✍️ **写下你的想法** — 让自己先答一遍，再看 AI 怎么答
📊 **思考记录** — 保存每次问题和想法，随时回看对照
📈 **统计面板** — 看看自己累计思考了多久
🌙 **暗色模式** — 自动适配系统主题

## 📝 Detailed Description (English)

**Are you outsourcing your brain to AI?**

A question pops up, you paste it into ChatGPT, you get a perfect answer. Done. But six months later, you realize — you've forgotten how to even *approach* a problem on your own.

**iThinkAI is a pause button for your brain.**

When you hit send on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any major AI platform, iThinkAI intercepts your message and opens a 10-second thinking window that asks:

> If *you* had to answer this, what would you say?

Write down your own thoughts first (optional), then let the AI respond. Compare the two — and watch your thinking muscle grow back.

---

### ✨ Features

🧠 **Think Timer** — A customizable 3-60 second pause before sending
✍️ **Write Your Thoughts** — Answer first, then let AI answer
📊 **Thinking Log** — Review your past thoughts and AI prompts
📈 **Stats Dashboard** — Track how much time you've reclaimed for thinking
🌙 **Dark Mode** — Respects your system theme
